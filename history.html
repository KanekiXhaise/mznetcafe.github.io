History

One of the best Cyber café in this little town of Gerona. Come and Explore the hospitable Gaming experience of its Management.

Marzone Internet Café is founded in January 2000 by Russel Marz Gazmin in Gerona,Tarlac.It had 10 64bit computers connected to PLDC service networks through telephone lines. Online service or offline meetings and Online Games were held in this place, which served as a place that connects a Players Heart to its Teammates.
